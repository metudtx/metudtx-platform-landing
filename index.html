Platform workflow

The platform structures the digital maturity assessment journey from company onboarding to expert analysis and roadmap outputs.

1

Company registration

Companies create their profile and submit the initial information required for the assessment process.

2

Self-assessment

The company completes the digital maturity self-assessment to establish the first view of its current state.

3

Expert analysis

METU DTX experts review the data, validate findings and assess maturity across strategic digital transformation dimensions.

4

Maturity reporting

The platform supports score-based analysis, observations, recommendations, executive summaries and detailed reports.

5

Roadmap planning

Priority project areas, transformation actions and roadmap elements are structured for implementation planning.

Expected outputs

The platform is designed to convert assessment evidence into actionable transformation outputs.

Digital maturity view

Structured analysis of current digital maturity, strengths, weaknesses and improvement potential.

Assessment report

Standardized reporting outputs including executive summary, axis-level findings and detailed recommendations.

Transformation roadmap

Prioritized project cards, implementation logic and roadmap elements for short-, medium- and long-term action.
