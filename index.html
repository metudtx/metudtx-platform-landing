A Structured Assessment-to-Roadmap Workflow

The platform structures the digital maturity assessment journey from company intake to expert validation, maturity analysis and transformation roadmap preparation.

1

Company Intake

Company profile, basic information, contact details and assessment scope are created in the platform.

2

Self-Assessment

The company completes the initial digital maturity self-assessment and provides a structured first view of its current state.

3

Expert Review

METU DTX experts validate submitted information through evidence review, interviews, observations and field assessment when applicable.

4

Maturity Analysis

The platform structures maturity scores, observations, strengths, weaknesses, recommendations and strategic findings.

5

Roadmap Planning

Priority transformation areas are converted into project cards, sequencing logic and a phased transformation roadmap.

Evidence-Based Digital Maturity Assessment

The assessment model combines company inputs, expert interpretation, maturity scoring and roadmap-oriented analysis. It is built to convert assessment evidence into transformation priorities.

Assessment model

The model evaluates companies through strategic transformation dimensions, category-level assessment and a 1–5 maturity logic based on validated evidence rather than self-declared ambition alone.
